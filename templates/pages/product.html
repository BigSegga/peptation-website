How to Use

For best results, follow this simple daily routine:

  • Step 1: Apply 5-6 drops of serum directly to clean, dry scalp
  • Step 2: Massage gently into scalp for 1-2 minutes to improve absorption
  • Step 3: Leave in — do not rinse. Style as usual
  • Step 4: Use once daily, preferably before bed

One bottle lasts approximately 30 days with daily use. For optimal results, we recommend using consistently for at least 90 days.
